The Sauce

The secret is in the sauce.

Homemade and delicious garlic sauce that greatly compliments the taste of the buns and dumplings. Please note: the sauce is quite robust and does not require a lot to have the desired effect.

Cut a small slit in the dumpling or bun and add a little sauce — or simply dip the product into the sauce.

Made fresh in Ponderay, Idaho — part of what makes us a one-of-a-kind stop for authentic Chinese food near Sandpoint.

"The secret is in the sauce."

— Roger, Owner
